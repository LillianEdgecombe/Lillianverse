Primeverse

The trunk from which everything branches. Three novels follow Lillian and Miles from their first meeting in 2003 through building a life, finding Sarah, and learning what a family actually is. Around the novels orbit short stories that fill in the quieter moments — a night moving in, a phone call at ten past ten, a daughter's voice memo to parents she misses.
