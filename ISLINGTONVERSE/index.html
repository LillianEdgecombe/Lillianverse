Islingtonverse

The texture of a shared life, told in episodes. Four seasons of domestic drama from the Islington house — structured like a TV series. No dramatic stakes, just the extraordinary weight of the completely ordinary: the chair that receives Lillian's weight with respect, the way Miles's ears flick when he's trying not to look worried.
