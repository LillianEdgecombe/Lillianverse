Welcome to the Lillianverse!

Have you ever tried to imagine how would your life have been if you took a different choice? And what if you lived in another time? Or were a different person entirely? If you said 'yes' to at least one of those questions, enter the Lillianverse!

In the Lillianverse, each story tells a different version of the same character, Lillian, each time with a different twist: a different occupation, a different life history, a different epoch, a different sex or even species. All stories are organized by theme.

Have fun and enjoy the differences!
