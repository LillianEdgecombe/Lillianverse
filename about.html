About the Lillianverse

Have you ever tried to imagine how your life would have been if you took a different choice? What if you lived in another time? Or were a different person entirely? If you said 'yes' to at least one of those questions, enter the Lillianverse!

In the Lillianverse, each story tells a different version of the same character, Lillian, each time with a different twist: a different occupation, a different life history, a different epoch, a different gender or even species. All stories are organized by theme to help you find exactly the kind of adventure you're looking for.

Have fun and enjoy exploring the infinite possibilities!

About the Project

The Lillianverse is a passion project exploring the infinite possibilities of storytelling through the lens of a single character experiencing countless different lives. Each universe represents a different genre, theme, or approach to narrative, allowing for both familiar and experimental storytelling.

This project celebrates the idea that every choice we make creates a new reality, and every story we tell opens a door to understanding ourselves and others in new ways.

Additionally, the project aims at developing a collaborative interface between human and artificial imagination and creativity, employing different AI models and algorithms to drive story development and outline - always retaining full creative control over the writing and always layering a final human touch to give each story its real "voice," something that no machine would ever be able to do.
