About the Lillianverse

◆

Have you ever tried to imagine how your life would have been if you'd made a different choice? What if you lived in another time? Were a different person entirely?

The Lillianverse began as a single story and became an obsession with one question: what would three specific people be, placed in any world the imagination can produce?

Three characters — a badger, a German Shepherd, and a golden retriever who became their daughter — living inside every story genre the human imagination has ever made. Victorian detective fiction. Famine-era Ireland. Byzantine Constantinople. Romantic comedy. Body horror. Mythic fantasy. Speculative AI fiction. And the quiet, ordinary drama of a shared life in Loughton, Essex.

Over sixty stories. Ten distinct worlds. And always the same stubborn conclusion: that warmth survives. That the people who make room for others leave marks that outlast everything else.

These stories are set in a world where anthropomorphic people have always existed — neither explained nor apologised for, simply present, navigating the same joys and cruelties as anyone.

The first novel, Venus in Furs, is available now. The trilogy continues.
