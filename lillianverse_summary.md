### 🌌 Lillianverse Canvas Summary

---

## 1. Core Concept
- Multi-layered universe of anthropomorphic characters.
- Themes: identity, belonging, art & culture, complex relationships, personal stakes.
- Tone: shifts from intimate slice-of-life to high-octane adventure.

---

## 2. Main Characters

### Lillian Edgecombe
- Species: Anthropomorphic European Badger, 42, curvy & solid.
- Roles: Music professor, band leader, potter, art model.
- Traits: Introverted, motherly, sensual, confident.
- Relationships: Married to Miles; adopted mother of Sarah.
- Narrative Themes: Body confidence, domestic power dynamics, personal loss, artistic influence.

### Miles Ferguson
- Species: Anthropomorphic German Shepherd, 42.
- Roles: Engineer, grocery store director.
- Traits: Competent, practical, loyal.
- Relationships: Husband to Lillian; father figure to Sarah.
- Narrative Themes: Competence vs. recklessness, loyalty, problem-solving.

### Sarah
- Species: Anthropomorphic Golden Retriever, 28.
- Traits: Wiry, artistic, independent, deeply attached to family.
- Background: Abandoned, adopted at 16.
- Narrative Themes: Adoption, resilience, artistic expression.

---

## 3. Universes & Continuity
- Primeverse: domestic, artistic, intimate slice-of-life.
- Techverse: sci-fi adventures, explorations of themes connected with technology.
- Kinkyverse: exploration of alternative erotic lifestyles: feederism, BDSM, dominance/passivity and others.
- Dramaverse: intimate themes exploring personal issues and challenges, with high dramatic component.
- Funverse: adventures with wit, banter and general lightweight themes, hitting new, weird and fantastic themes and settings.
- Historyverse: explorations of the characters in different moments of the historical timeline, from the Neolithic to modern times.
- Horrorverse: exploration of themes and settings with a horror component.
- Weirdverse: adventures in complex, abnormal realities.
- Mysteryverse: noir/mystery/spy and similar stories.

---

## 4. Key Narrative Devices
- Interspecies dynamics.
- Subtle kink/power dynamics in personal rituals.
- Personal stakes drive action.
- Art/music as plot and metaphor.
- Character flexibility: both can be present or just one of them, they can be represented by different species or sex, genderswap and species swap allowed, exploring other body shapes or identities.
