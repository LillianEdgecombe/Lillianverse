The Main Station

Welcome to the heart of the Lillianverse! Each door leads to a different themed universe where Lillian lives countless different lives. Choose your destination:

Available Universes

Primeverse

The original timeline - where it all begins. Follow Lillian's foundational story and discover the core of her character.

Dramaverse

Stories filled with emotional depth and complex relationships. Explore the human condition through Lillian's dramatic experiences.

Funverse

Light-hearted adventures and comedic tales. Join Lillian in her more whimsical and entertaining journeys.

Historyverse

Lillian across different historical periods. Experience how she might have lived in various eras throughout time.

Horroverse

Dark tales and supernatural encounters. Venture into the shadows with Lillian's more frightening adventures.

Kinkyverse

Adult-oriented stories exploring mature themes. (18+ content)

Techverse

Science fiction and futuristic scenarios. Discover Lillian's adventures in technological and sci-fi settings.

Weirdverse

Surreal and experimental narratives. Enter the strange and unconventional sides of Lillian's existence.

Navigation Tips

Each universe contains multiple stories exploring different aspects of that theme. You can always return here using the navigation menu, or explore the connections between universes as they develop.

New to the Lillianverse? Start with the Primeverse to understand Lillian's core character, then branch out to whichever themes interest you most.
