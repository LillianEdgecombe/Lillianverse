Character Artwork

Lillian Edgecombe

An anthropomorphic European Badger in her early forties. A talented musician and ceramicist, Lillian is known for her strong presence, deliberate movements, and protective nature. She and her husband Miles have adopted Sarah, creating a loving found family.

Miles Ferguson

An anthropomorphic German Shepherd in his early forties. Miles works as a grocery store manager and is characterized by his loyalty, methodical approach to problems, and quiet strength. His relationship with Lillian forms the emotional core of their household.

Sarah

The adopted daughter of Lillian and Miles, a Golden Retriever, Sarah is a talented artist in her late twenties. She struggles with her lack of childhood memories but finds her voice through art, creating powerful works that explore themes of identity, memory, and belonging.
