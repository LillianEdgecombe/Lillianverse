# Lillianverse — Distribution Plan
*Last updated: March 2026*

## Key
- 🌐 **Free** — on-site, no account needed
- 🟠 **Wattpad** — external link, free with account
- 📗 **Ko-fi** — subscriber exclusive ($3 or $6/month)
- 📦 **KDP** — Amazon eBook (paid)
- ✅ **Live** — already published/linked

Novels with a Wattpad preview follow the rule: **first 5 chapters on Wattpad, full work on KDP**.
Islingtonverse seasons follow the same rule: **first 5 episodes on Wattpad, full season on KDP**.
All Kinkyverse content: **Ko-fi only** (adult content, no exceptions).
